Chaman Bazar

Chaman Bazar is a specialized government digital initiative designed to regulate and monitor the prices of essential commodities specifically within Chaman city. This project serves as a transparent bridge between the administration and the public, ensuring that official price lists are implemented and accessible to all citizens. By providing real-time data on the costs of daily goods, Chaman Bazar aims to curb overcharging, stabilize the local market, and empower residents with a reliable tool for consumer rights and price accountability.
